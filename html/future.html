Tracks

Key Tracks to Unlock Value

Execution across infrastructure, capability and services.

Nationwide Fiber & IXPs

Last‑mile fiber in metros and shared backhaul to tier‑2 cities; local peering to lower latency.

5G Standalone Pilots

Campus networks, FWA for SMEs and industrial IoT sandboxes in ports and SEZs.

AI for Public Services

Voice bots for citizen services, OCR for records, and AI triage in tele‑health.

Fintech & Open Payments

Interoperable rails, QR ubiquity and SME credit scoring to lift digital commerce.

Skills & Startup Pipeline

National micro‑credentials, cloud credits and co‑working near campuses.

Rights & Safety by Design

Transparent content rules, due process and data protection to build trust.

Possible Future

Three Plausible Futures

Direction hinges on spectrum policy, investment and rights guarantees.

Green Sprint

Co-ordinated spectrum, fiber and talent programs deliver inclusive growth.

Patchy Progress

Selective metro focus; slow rights clarity; affordability pressure persists.

Stalled Momentum

Fragmented policy and limited capex produce stagnation across services.
